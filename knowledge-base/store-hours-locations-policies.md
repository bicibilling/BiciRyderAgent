## Store Policies

### Service & Repair Policies
- **Appointment Booking:** 24-hour advance booking recommended
- **Walk-in Service:** Limited same-day availability (first-come, first-served)
- **Service Guarantee:** 30-day warranty on all repairs
- **Turnaround Time:** 
  - Basic tune-up: 1-2 business days
  - Major repairs: 3-7 business days
  - Custom builds: 2-3 weeks

### Payment Policies
**Accepted Payment Methods:**
- Cash
- All major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex)
- Debit cards (Interac)
- Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Store financing available (12-month 0% APR)

### Return & Exchange Policies
**Bicycles:**
- 30-day return policy with original receipt
- Bike must be in like-new condition
- 15% restocking fee applies
- Custom orders are final sale

**Accessories & Parts:**
- 14-day return policy
- Original packaging required
- No returns on safety items (helmets, lights) once used

**Service & Repairs:**
- 30-day satisfaction guarantee
- Free adjustments within 30 days of service
- Parts warranty varies by manufacturer (90 days to 2 years)

### Warranty Policies
**New Bike Warranties:**
- Frame: Lifetime (original owner)
- Components: 1-2 years (varies by manufacturer)
- Labor: 90 days from purchase date
- Free tune-up within 30 days of purchase

**Used Bike Warranties:**
- 90-day warranty on major components
- 30-day labor warranty
- No warranty on wear items (tires, brake pads, chains)

### COVID-19 Safety Protocols
- Hand sanitizer available at entrance
- Mask wearing recommended but not required
- Contact-free payment options preferred
- Appointment booking encouraged for service
- Enhanced cleaning protocols between services

### Customer Service Policies
- **Price Matching:** We match local competitor prices on identical items
- **Test Rides:** Available for all bikes with valid ID
- **Bike Fitting:** Complimentary basic fitting with purchase
- **Professional Fitting:** Available by appointment ($75, applied to bike purchase)

### Special Services
- **Home Delivery:** Available within 20km ($25 fee)
- **Pick-up & Delivery:** For service appointments ($40 round-trip)
- **Corporate Accounts:** Volume discounts available
- **Group Rides:** Organized every Saturday 9 AM (all skill levels)

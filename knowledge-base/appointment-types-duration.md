## Service Preparation

### What to Bring
**All Appointments:**
- Valid photo ID
- Bike (clean preferred)
- Keys/locks for security systems
- List of specific concerns or issues

**Fitting Appointments:**
- Cycling shoes and preferred clothing
- Current measurements if available
- Any injury or comfort concerns

**Warranty Service:**
- Original purchase receipt
- Warranty documentation
- Description of issue

### Before Your Appointment
- Remove all accessories (lights, computers, bags)
- Clean bike if possible (helps with inspection)
- Make note of specific issues or concerns
- Confirm appointment 24 hours prior

### Service Expectations
**Drop-off Time:** Arrive 10 minutes early for paperwork
**Pickup Time:** Flexible within business hours
**Communication:** Updates provided for services over $100
**Test Ride:** Included with all major services

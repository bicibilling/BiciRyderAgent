## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you offer test rides?**
A: Yes! Test rides are available for all bikes. Please bring ID and we'll set you up.

**Q: Can you order specific models?**
A: Absolutely! We can special order any bike from our partner brands. Typical delivery is 1-2 weeks.

**Q: Do you service bikes not purchased from BICI?**
A: Yes, we service all bike brands and models.

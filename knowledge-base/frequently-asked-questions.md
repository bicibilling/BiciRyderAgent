# BICI Bike Store - Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### Q: What types of bikes do you sell?
**A:** We specialize in:
- **Road Bikes:** Racing, endurance, gravel, and touring bikes
- **Mountain Bikes:** Cross-country, trail, all-mountain, and downhill
- **Electric Bikes:** Commuter, mountain, cargo, and folding e-bikes  
- **Hybrid Bikes:** Commuter, fitness, and comfort bikes
- **Kids Bikes:** Balance bikes, 12" to 24" wheel sizes
- **Specialty:** Cargo bikes, folding bikes, single-speed, and fixies

### Q: Do you sell used bikes?
**A:** Yes! We have a curated selection of quality used bikes that have been inspected and serviced by our mechanics. All used bikes come with a 90-day warranty on major components.

### Q: Can I test ride a bike before buying?
**A:** Absolutely! We encourage test rides for all bikes. Just bring a valid photo ID and we'll set you up. Test rides are available during all business hours.

### Q: Do you offer bike fitting services?
**A:** Yes, we offer two levels:
- **Basic Fit:** Complimentary with any bike purchase (saddle height, handlebar position)
- **Professional Fit:** $75 comprehensive fitting (credited toward bike purchase over $500)

## Service & Repairs

### Q: How long do repairs take?
**A:** Typical turnaround times:
- **Basic Tune-up:** 1-2 business days
- **Brake Adjustment:** Same day (if parts available)
- **Flat Tire Repair:** 15-30 minutes
- **Derailleur Adjustment:** 30-60 minutes
- **Major Overhaul:** 3-7 business days

### Q: Do I need an appointment for service?
**A:** Appointments are recommended and guarantee service time. We do accept walk-ins based on availability, but wait times may vary.

### Q: What's included in a tune-up?
**A:** Our standard tune-up includes:
- Brake adjustment and cable inspection
- Derailleur adjustment and indexing  
- Wheel truing and spoke tension check
- Chain cleaning and lubrication
- Tire pressure and wear inspection
- All bolts torqued to specification
- Safety inspection and test ride

### Q: Do you work on all bike brands?
**A:** Yes, our certified mechanics work on all brands and types of bikes, including e-bikes. Some specialty or vintage bikes may require special ordering of parts.

### Q: What if I'm not satisfied with a repair?
**A:** We guarantee all work for 30 days. If you're not satisfied, we'll make it right at no charge.

## Electric Bikes

### Q: Do you service e-bikes?
**A:** Yes! Our mechanics are trained and certified to work on all major e-bike systems including Bosch, Shimano, Brose, and Yamaha.

### Q: What's the range on your e-bikes?
**A:** Range varies by model and riding conditions, typically:
- **Commuter E-bikes:** 40-80 km per charge
- **Mountain E-bikes:** 30-60 km per charge  
- **Cargo E-bikes:** 50-100 km per charge

### Q: How long do e-bike batteries last?
**A:** Modern e-bike batteries typically last 3-5 years or 500-1000 charge cycles with proper care.

### Q: Can I ride an e-bike in the rain?
**A:** Yes, all our e-bikes are weather-resistant (IPX4 rating or higher), but avoid deep water and always store indoors when possible.

## Accessories & Safety

### Q: What safety gear do you recommend?
**A:** Essential safety gear:
- **Helmet:** CSA or CPSC certified (required by Ontario law for under 18)
- **Lights:** Front white light and rear red light for visibility
- **Bell:** Required by law in most municipalities
- **Reflective gear:** For low-light riding

### Q: Do you install accessories?
**A:** Yes! We install all accessories we sell, including:
- Lights, bells, and computers
- Racks, fenders, and water bottle cages
- Child seats and trailers
- Locks and security systems

### Q: What's the best lock for my bike?
**A:** We recommend:
- **U-locks:** Best security for high-value bikes
- **Chain locks:** Good flexibility for multiple bikes
- **Folding locks:** Balance of security and portability
- **Cable locks:** Only for low-risk, short-term parking

## Seasonal Questions

### Q: When should I bring my bike in for service?
**A:** We recommend:
- **Spring:** Pre-season tune-up after winter storage
- **Mid-season:** Quick safety check and cleaning
- **Fall:** End-of-season service before winter storage

### Q: How should I store my bike in winter?
**A:** Indoor storage tips:
- Clean and lubricate chain
- Inflate tires to proper pressure
- Store in dry location away from heat sources
- Consider removing battery from e-bikes

### Q: Do you rent bikes?
**A:** We don't offer rentals, but we can recommend several local rental shops for tourists and occasional riders.

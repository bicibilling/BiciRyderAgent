# BICI Bike Store - Repair Services & Pricing

## Service Packages

### Basic Tune-Up - $75
**Recommended:** Every 6 months or 1,000km
**Turnaround:** 1-2 business days

**Includes:**
- Brake adjustment and cable inspection
- Derailleur adjustment and indexing
- Chain cleaning and lubrication
- Tire pressure check and adjustment
- All bolts torqued to manufacturer specifications
- Safety inspection and test ride
- **Free:** Within 30 days of bike purchase

### Comprehensive Tune-Up - $125
**Recommended:** Annually or 2,000km
**Turnaround:** 2-3 business days

**Includes everything in Basic Tune-Up plus:**
- Wheel truing and spoke tension adjustment
- Headset adjustment and bearing inspection
- Bottom bracket inspection and adjustment
- Hub bearing inspection and adjustment
- Frame and component deep cleaning
- Cable housing replacement (if needed)
- Brake pad inspection and adjustment

### Complete Overhaul - $250
**Recommended:** Every 2-3 years or 5,000km
**Turnaround:** 5-7 business days

**Includes everything in Comprehensive Tune-Up plus:**
- Complete disassembly and cleaning
- All bearing services (headset, bottom bracket, hubs)
- Cable and housing replacement
- New chain (if within stretch limit)
- Brake pad replacement
- Bar tape replacement (road bikes)
- Full component inspection and replacement recommendations

## Maintenance Tips & Recommendations

### Regular Maintenance Schedule
**Weekly (heavy use) / Monthly (casual use):**
- Check tire pressure
- Clean and lube chain
- Check brake function
- Inspect tires for wear/debris

**Every 3-6 Months:**
- Basic tune-up recommended
- Brake pad inspection
- Cable tension check

**Annually:**
- Comprehensive tune-up
- Bearing inspection
- Full safety inspection

### Seasonal Service Reminders
**Spring:** Post-winter tune-up and safety check
**Summer:** Mid-season inspection and cleaning
**Fall:** Pre-winter service and storage prep

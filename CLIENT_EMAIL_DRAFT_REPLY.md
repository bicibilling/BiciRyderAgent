## Current Setup (What's Working Now)

Your AI agent is using **Shopify's public product catalog** to look up inventory. This is the same data that's available on your website at bici.cc - no special app or credentials needed.

**How it works:**
- When a customer asks about products, the agent searches your published Shopify products in real-time
- It finds matches based on product titles, types, brands, and tags
- Returns product details including prices, sizes, and availability

**What's being accessed:**
- Product names and descriptions
- Prices and variants (sizes/colors)
- Availability status
- Product URLs

This is all public information - the same data anyone can see browsing your website.

## About the Shopify App

You're right that we mentioned setting up a Shopify app and API credentials. That was intended for **future enhancements**, specifically:

**Draft Orders:** The app/credentials would allow the AI to:
- Create draft orders in Shopify when customers want to buy
- Generate real checkout links
- Track orders

**Better Inventory Access:** Could provide more detailed stock counts beyond "available/unavailable"

## Do You Need to Set Up the App Now?

**Short answer: No, not urgent.**

The current inventory lookup works well for helping customers discover products and get information. The AI can:
- ✅ Search your catalog
- ✅ Show product details and pricing
- ✅ Answer questions about availability
- ✅ Share product page links for purchase

When customers are ready to buy, the AI currently:
- Shares the direct product page link (bici.cc/products/...)
- Transfers them to your team for purchase assistance
- Provides your phone number for orders

## If You Want Enhanced Features

If you'd like the AI to be able to create draft orders or provide more detailed inventory information, then we'd want to set up the Shopify app. But for basic product discovery and recommendations, the current setup works great without it.
